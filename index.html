WELCOME

Moving home can be one of the most stressful times in your life. Here at Pro Property Care we strive to make this transition as smooth as possible for both tenants and landlords. Not only for the short-term but throughout the whole tenancy.

We are a locally based independent family business, with over 20 years’ experience in local Property Management and Maintenance industry.

We operate our lettings management services in the local area, which allows us to prioritise and respond to our tenants and landlords in a timely manner with regular scheduled visits and ongoing maintenance support. By being genuinely pro-active in this way, we can respond to issues quickly and efficiently and ensure the tenants have full confidence in the support they receive from us and ensuring the tenancy runs smoothly.

Our modern approach to letting and management combines a traditional format with the benefit of digital online forms, applications and e-signing making the whole process much more convenient, simple and is usually much faster than most agents.
