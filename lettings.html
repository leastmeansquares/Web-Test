Digital Applications & Processes

We operate our communications and applications online and do not rely on traditional systems such as snail-mail and tedious paperwork to slow the process down. Our dynamic application process allows tenants to immediately submit their interest by filling out our bespoke digital forms and upload documents from their desktop, tablet or mobile phone, allowing us to process the tenant’s application with the minimum of fuss.

Advantages of our Digital Applications & Processes

Main Advantages are:  Digital Applications from your choice of electronic device.  E-Signature Contracts (no waiting for post or presence).  No printing (great for the environment).  Email automated confirmation & prompts throughout the application process.
