LETTINGS AND MANAGEMENT

A FASTER, MORE DYNAMIC WAY OF FINDING TENANTS & MANAGING PROPERTIES

Independent Residential Lettings

We manage our letting services locally and in-house so we can get to work immediately without the delays from third parties.

Online Property Management

Our ongoing online support and hands-on maintenance assistance is key to keeping your tenants happy and the property in good working order. Real time updates and prompt responses mean you can always be confident that your property is in safe hands.

Property Listing Within 24 Hours

With our online advertising, branded To-Let Board, and professional photographs, cut out the long delays in making your property visible. We aim to list all our properties within 24 hours.

Easy Digital Applications

Our easy online application process allow tenants to quickly submit forms and documents from any device, allowing us to process applications without the tedious paperwork, snail-mail, or fuss.

Seamless Property Transfers

Transfer properties without the fuss and carry over your existing payment dates and bonds. Our price match guarantee ensures we will match or better your existing terms.

A Service Personalised to You

We tailor our landlord services to the landlord so you only need to pay for the bits you need. Whether you’re a seasoned professional or a complete novice, we can tailor a service that will fit what you're looking for.
