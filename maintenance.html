Services

We have a wide range of services available and can usually accommodate all jobs whether they are large or small. Listed below are some of the services we offer but we will do our best to address any maintenance issues you may encounter.


A Personal Service

Because we are a small local business, we are able to offer a personal service, with attention to detail, whilst at the same time maintaining a high standard of competence and professionalism.


Competitive Rates

Our fees are competitive and may be open to negotiation dependent on the level of Service required. We are particularly committed to ensuring none of our customers are forgotten, no matter what size of job is required. We also offer discounted rates to senior citizens.


Our Customers

As we are a local residential business, our main customers are the general public and we also complete work for landlords and letting agents.

Types of Service Offered

  • Services to General Public
  • Landlords & Letting Agents Service

We will normally identify the work required over the phone or on a visit where we can provide a FREE quotation for any work required.

Once any work has been agreed, we will book a convenient date to commence.

On completion, payments can be made in the following ways- cash, bank transfer or by card (small charge).

We reach out far and wide to landlords who may be local, national or overseas. We also complete work for letting agents on behalf of some landlords.

We appreciate that as a landlord (or agent) you may not be present at the property when work is carried out and therefore, we ensure that timely feedback and evidence is supplied by the contractor to keep your records up to date and all necessary parties in the loop. We also provide prompt online invoicing.

On completion, payments can be made in the following ways- cash, bank transfer or by card (small charge). We understand that small delays may be relevant for making payments from rental income.

Free Quotations

We offer Free "no obligation" Quotations and competitive rates for work carried out.

Services Listed (below)

We have listed details of some of the most frequently requested services below.

Repairs

This will generally be categorized as a small job and charges as a call-out or hourly rate (normally agreed before commencement). Don't forget.....we normally undertake the smaller jobs at the same time as the bigger jobs so it can all be done at the same time which can normally save time and money. Please ask about this if you have multiple jobs that need completing.

Handyman Services (Single or Multiple Jobs)

This category can be varied so it would probably require attendance beforehand to determine a price. Small repairs are often undertaken with other services like decorating or whilst in attendance for another job (skills permitting) which can often save money on additional trades person call-outs.

Locksmiths Upvc/Lock Replacement/Repairs

The most common types of issues are normally faulty locks and window handles and replacement windows.

Landscape Gardening, Fencing/Repairs, Patios, Lawns, Weed/Shrub Removal (Flattening)

Bespoke landscape designs, new fence (or post/panel repairs), patios, decking, lawns, hedges, etc

Joinery & Plastering

Laminate flooring, skirting, architraves, doors to name a few. Plastering, sealing/painting/finishing......complete jobs. Preventative damp-proofing- dry-lining, membranes etc.

Tiling & Plumbing

Whether it's a full bathroom or a bit of splash-back tiling on the sink. Tiled steps or hearths- bespoke work. General plumbing.

Guttering Cleans, Shed/Summerhouse Roof Re-covers (& General External Maintenance)

Blocked guttering will normally cause internal damp on your property (or a neighbours). The first sign of any blockage, you should have them cleaned. Why not have them cleaned every year as part of a maintenance contract. We can recover sheds/Summerhouse roofs with lightweight materials (boarding and shingles). All external general maintenance is undertaken.
