IMPORTANT APPLICATIONS AND PROCESSES

CUT THE HASSLE OUT OF FINDING A HOME

The Application Process

Booking a Viewing
Before arranging a viewing on a property, you will be asked a series of questions to help us assess your eligibility to apply for a property you are interested in. It is important that information given is accurate as this will determine your eligibility not only to view the property but also in being successful in any future application.
Viewing Details
We will normally try to arrange viewings individually but sometimes we may need to arrange consecutive (20-minute increments) or block viewings. Regardless of the number of viewers, it is recommended that you register your interest in the property as soon as possible thereafter. A rental guide will be provided available on request or on the website.
Making an Application
Please inform us of your intention to apply for the property. You will be sent an online application form and detailed information and links about the tenancy. Please ensure you have all the supporting documentation ready which can be uploaded via your application form.

Our online digital forms allow our prospective tenants to submit their online applications and uploads much faster than a traditional process, resulting in quicker decisions and less time wasted or a property empty for longer. Likewise, all our forms can be completed and signed online which benefits the whole letting process from start to finish.

Please note - a holding deposit will also be required at this stage.
Referencing, Checks, & Criteria
The following criteria will be assessed:

Credit Check Affordability Check Employer's Reference
Previous Landlord's Reference Rental History Check Right to Rent Check
IMPORTANT: The Holding Deposit may be retained if any of the following conditions apply

- You supply false or misleading information (withholding can be misleading).

- You fail a Right-To-Rent check.

- You fail to take all reasonable steps to enter into a Tenancy Agreement (thereafter).

Should any of the above conditions apply which results in the holding deposit being retained, you will be notified of this promptly with a full explanation. If a holding deposit is to be returned at this stage, this will also be done promptly.
Further Referencing Details
Guarantors:

A Guarantor may be a pre-requisite for application for some tenants and would need to go through the referencing system with regards to acceptance and should be employed and/or homeowner.

A Guarantor may potentially still be accepted if the referencing criteria fails, however, if it is suspected prior to applying, this should be discussed before applying as no guarantee will be given that a landlord will accept a Guarantor retrospectively, resulting in rejection. Cases will be assessed independently.

Holding Deposit:

A holding deposit of one-weeks rent will be payable immediately on application. The property will be put on hold (up to 15 days) until the referencing is completed. No further applications will be processed during this period. If the application is successful, you will then need to pay a Security Deposit (Bond) for the property and sign a Tenancy Agreement, along with additional tenants and any guarantors. The bond amount is equivalent to five-weeks rent.
Successful Application & Check-In
A successful application will be confirmed in writing along with the next steps, commitments and timescales. We will work to provisional dates given in your application (unless other dates are agreed).

We will prepare Tenancy Agreements, Inventory & Property Condition Reports and book an appointment at the property to complete a physical check-in (in person). By doing this we are able to discuss and assist with any questions you may have and sign any agreements or Inventory that may not have been signed up to this point. If anyone has difficulty attending check-in day, we can work round this so long as we know in advance.
